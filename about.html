Meet the Artist

Hello! I'm Shravani Phutane, an independent artist with a passion for handcrafted expressions of beauty. From childhood doodles to exhibitions across the country, I’ve grown into someone who believes that every handmade piece carries a soul.

My goal is to bring warmth, uniqueness, and sustainability to your spaces through art made with love and patience.
